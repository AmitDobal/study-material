Flows: 
    1. Entry Flow
        Vehicle will arrive. Generate a ticket. Assign a parking slot and floor.
    2. Exit Flow
        Calculate the fee. do payment. Update the parking slot. Generate the reciept. mark the ticket inActive.
    3. Admin Flow
        CRUD operations related to - Parking Slots, Payments etc
